About Me

I’m a user experience designer based in McLean, Virginia and currently residing in Chapel Hill, North Carolina.

I graduated Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology in 2023 and am currently pursuing a double major in Information Science and Computer Science at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill with an expected graduation in 2026.

I love designing and creating even outside of work. My favorite pastimes include playing my favorite songs on the piano, composing music, playing with my dog, and drawing.
